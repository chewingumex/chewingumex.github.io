// Background

I'm a Lead Data Scientist and team leader with a PhD from University College London and a background in psychology — a combination that shapes how I frame problems, communicate insight, and build teams that think carefully. I believe the best data science is always in service of a real question.

I've led data science teams at HSBC (12 scientists, CIB growth), Warner Music Group (Director of Audience Targeting), and Freemavens (Head of DS). Across FinTech, AdTech, Market Research, and FMCG, I've built and shipped ML solutions — from credit scorecards and audience segments to generative AI pipelines and time series forecasts.

I also lecture in Business Intelligence at ITESM Campus Sta Fe, teaching the next generation how to apply machine learning to commercial problems. I learn fast, play fair, and leave things better than I found them.

// The Thinking

Psychometrics led me to data science. A PhD in psychoanalytic studies gave me an obsession with what's hidden beneath the surface — which turns out to be exactly the right mindset for modelling. I enjoy the full stack: shaping the problem, building the model, deploying it, and mentoring the team around it. Self-teaching and open source have been central to everything I know. As long as I keep learning with a busy mind, I'll be happy.
