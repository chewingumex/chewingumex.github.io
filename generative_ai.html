// Overview

Design and implementation of ARTy — an Automated Review Text pipeline able to ingest reviews, news articles and social media posts and generate synthetic summaries of their most salient attributes. Built and deployed for clients including L'Oréal and Shell, the system extracts brand sentiment, key themes and actionable insights at scale. The work also involved training custom LSTM and Attention-based Transformer architectures for summarisation, translation and sequence-to-sequence tasks.
