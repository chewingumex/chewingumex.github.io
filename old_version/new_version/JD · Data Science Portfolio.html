About

I'm JD — a data scientist based in London, UK. This portfolio is a living record of the problems I find interesting enough to spend time on. Projects range from consumer confidence in Mexico to movie segmentation to credit risk modeling.

I believe good data science is as much about asking the right questions as it is about the models you build. Each project ends with open questions for future iterations — because there's always more to explore.

Feel free to share the code, fork the repos, or reach out with suggestions. I'm always up for a conversation about data.
